Tool I built: Rentgen.io

I don’t wait for the “perfect” tool. When something is missing, I build it. That’s how Rentgen appeared.

Rentgen – see what you should test, not just what you can send

Rentgen takes a single API request and generates multiple tests around it: boundaries, weird values, enums, ranges and negative cases.

It’s designed for testers and developers who care about risk, not about spending their day clicking through a pretty REST client.

Built by me. Free local version, with a clear line between open-source and commercial use.
